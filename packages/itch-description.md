# Paste into itch.io → Edit project → Description

Guide a meditator through 7 ascending layers of mind. Fill the Practice bar and reach 80% Mindfulness to advance. Collect bright golden Scriptures for shields and Mindfulness. From Layer 2 you take flight in all four directions. Three gentle reminders invite you to pause and breathe — a calm game about focus, not frenzy.

## How to play

**Desktop:** Arrow keys / WASD to move, Space to jump, click for Pulse.

**Mobile:** Works best in **portrait** on iPhone; **landscape** on some Android devices. If the layout looks wrong after rotating, refresh the page.

## Cách chơi trên điện thoại

- **iPhone:** chơi ổn nhất khi **giữ dọc** (portrait).
- **Android (một số máy):** chơi ổn nhất khi **ngang** (landscape).
- Nếu xoay màn hình bị lệch layout: **tải lại trang** (refresh).
